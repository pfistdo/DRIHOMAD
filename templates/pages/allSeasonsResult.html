Home advantage calculation from all Formula 1 seasons

Explanation

The home advantage is calculated with a ratio. The ratio is calculated by comparing every drivers average placement of a season to the placement of his home grandprix.
We then calculate the percentage of drivers who performed better at home.

This means in short:
Home advantage ratio = All drivers with a better placement at home in one season / Total drivers with home grandprix in that season.
